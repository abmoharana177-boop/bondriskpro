What is Duration?
Duration measures how sensitive a bond's price is to interest rate changes.

Higher duration = higher risk when interest rates change.
A 1% rate rise drops price by ~—.
Duration vs Maturity
Duration is always less than maturity.
Higher market rate → shorter duration.
Formula Used
Duration = Maturity ÷ (1 + Market Rate)
Simple Macaulay Approx.
